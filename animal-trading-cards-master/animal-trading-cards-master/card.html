Owl

Owls have exceptional night vision, thanks to their large, forward-facing eyes.

  • Scientific name: Strigidae
  • Average length: 70 centimeters
  • Average lifespan: 20 years
  • Max speed: 64 kilometers per hour

Owls are magnificent creatures of the night, captivating with their unique features and behaviors. With their large, forward-facing eyes, they possess exceptional night vision, allowing them to navigate through darkness with ease. Their silent flight, aided by specialized feathers, enables them to approach their prey undetected.
